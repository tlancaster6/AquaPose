<!-- YOLO pose label format -->
Pose label: `cls cx cy w h x1 y1 v1 x2 y2 v2 ...` (all normalized [0,1], v=2 for visible)
OBB label: `cls x1 y1 x2 y2 x3 y3 x4 y4` (all normalized [0,1])
6 keypoints: nose, head, spine1, spine2, spine3, tail
