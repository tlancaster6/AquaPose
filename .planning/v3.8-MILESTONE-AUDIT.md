# v3.8 Improved Association — Milestone Audit

## Milestone Summary

**Goal:** Replace single-centroid ray scoring with multi-keypoint association, add swap-aware group validation and singleton recovery, and tune on real data to reduce the ~27% singleton rate.

**Result:** Singleton rate reduced from 27% (v3.7) to 5.4% (v3.8) — an 80% reduction, far exceeding the ~15% target. Reprojection error improved from ~3.0px to 2.85px. Improvement is entirely architectural, not parameter-driven.
