Specific additions:

**Section 6 — Stage 1 (Detection):**
- Note that YOLO-OBB `(v2.2)` will be a configurable detection model producing oriented bounding boxes with rotation angle and OBB polygon points
- Affine crop utilities `(v2.2)` will support rotation-aligned crops and back-projection from crop to frame coordinates

**Section 6 — Stage 4 (Midline):**
- Expand the direct pose estimation bullet with the keypoint approach `(v2.2)`:
  - U-Net encoder (frozen, from segmentation weights) + regression head → 6 anatomical keypoints
  - Each keypoint has a fixed `t` value in [0,1] calibrated from full skeletons (mean cumulative arc-length fractions)
  - Output is always `np.linspace(0, 1, N)` — point #k always means the same anatomical location
  - Fit spline using only observed keypoints and their fixed t values
  - Evaluate spline only within `[t_min_observed, t_max_observed]`; everything outside → NaN + confidence=0 (no extrapolation)
  - Per-point confidence from the regression head flows to reconstruction for weighted triangulation

**Section 6 — Stage 5 (Reconstruction):**
- Note that both triangulation and curve optimizer backends will support confidence-weighted observations `(v2.2)` — per-point confidence from the keypoint backend, uniform weights when confidence is None

**Section 11 (Configuration):**

**Section 14 (CLI):**
