## Decisions Made

- **OBB model approved:** mAP50-95=0.781 vs Round 1 0.707 — +7.4 pts improvement. Clear win, no caveats.
- **Pose model approved:** mAP50-95=0.974 vs Round 1 0.968 — +0.6 pts. Comparison is conservative (harder 128-image all-source val vs Round 1's 64-image manual-only val), so the improvement is meaningful.
- **White-wall recall deferred:** Marginal improvement observed in investigation video but edge cases remain. Not blocking Phase 80+ tracker work; deferred to future domain adaptation or additional annotation work.
- **False positive above tank accepted:** One detection above tank ROI at conf=0.618 visible in video. Will be naturally filtered by triangulation geometry and association stage — no match in other cameras means it can't form a 3D point. Tank ROI mask deferred to future phase.
- **Pose model comparison note:** The val set changed between Round 1 (64-image manual-only) and production retrain (128-image manual+corrected all-source). Direct numeric comparison is informative but not apples-to-apples. Model competence confirmed, not just metric improvement.
