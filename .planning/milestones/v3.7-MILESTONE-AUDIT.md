## ASSOC-01 Gap Analysis

**Root cause:** Phase 82 implemented keypoint centroid extraction in `ocsort_wrapper._TrackletBuilder.add_frame()`. Phase 85 deleted `ocsort_wrapper.py` entirely as part of BoxMot removal. The `KeypointTracker` (Phase 83) was designed independently and uses KF-predicted keypoint positions for centroids, never consulting the `centroid_keypoint_index` config.

**Impact:** Cross-view association still uses centroids, but they come from Kalman filter prediction of all 6 keypoints rather than the empirically-selected mid-body keypoint (spine1, index 2). The quality difference depends on how much the KF-predicted center-of-mass differs from the spine1 position.

**Fix complexity:** Low — add keypoint selection logic to `KeypointTracker._TrackletBuilder` (the equivalent of what was in `ocsort_wrapper._TrackletBuilder`). The config threading is already complete.
